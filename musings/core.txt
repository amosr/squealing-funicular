Idle play adventures guild game

player starts with 1 adventurer
player can manually make adventurer fight for xp by clicking "fight" (rapid click) when not away
player can send adventurer to quest earning gold and xp (cooldown)
player can send adventurer to adventure finding items (cooldown)
Player can purchase tallent scouts which at intervals have the chance to find more heros, shit ones might be free, better ones might need one off payment?

PVP
player can group adventurers into partys of upto 6
Partys can quest and adventure as per above
Partys can also raid. Raid searches for other parties and attacks them potentially killing them, winners take the loot


adventurer's
Classes
Bard - helps recruit new people
Fighter - Attacks and defends in PVP
Rogue - helps hide party from other partys and find other parties
Wizard - Do DPS to "fight" and in PVP
Cleric - Heals
